Bharat Forge is providing free soil and silt to farmers in Udachiwadi as part of its ongoing water conservation efforts in the village. As part of the 2024-25 Rural Development Scheme, Bharat Forge Ltd., Pune, provided essential financial and technical support to launch a community-focused agricultural initiative. This program included a mango tree distribution and plantation ceremony aimed at enhancing both the local environment and supporting sustainable agricultural practices. The event was held on Saturday, 3rd August 2024, at 9:00 AM at the Gram Panchayat Office in Udachiwadi.
