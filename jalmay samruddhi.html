Empowering Villages & Creating Prosperous Rural Communities

Mission

A Drought-Free and Prosperous Maharashtra


For decades, drought has ravaged thousands of villages in Maharashtra, leading to thirst, hunger, debt, forced migration and withered crops. After travelling through hundreds of villages, and speaking with veterans in the field, we discovered that this crisis is largely man-made. It is a result of mismanagement of water and natural resources, exacerbated by the uncertainties caused by climate change. The scientific solutions to eradicating this crisis can be found in methods of water conservation, water management and environment restoration. These are well-known solutions, propagated by experts for years.

  • Continuous Contour Trenches : The Paani Foundation has built continuous contour trenches (CCTs) as part of the Satyamev Jayate Water Cup initiative. These trenches help reduce soil erosion and conserve water in semi-arid regions, promoting sustainable agriculture and community resilience

  • The Paani Foundation has created percolation tanks and farm ponds that improve groundwater recharge and provide essential water resources for farming.

  • The Paani Foundation has actively engaged in tree plantation efforts in Udachiwadi, enhancing biodiversity and supporting environmental sustainability in the region.
