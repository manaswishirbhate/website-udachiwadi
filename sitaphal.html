Sitaphal thrive in hot and dry climates .This makes them ideal for places like udachiwadi ,where the weather is warm and there are long period without rain. These trees can tolerate drought , meaning they can grow well even when water is scares. Sitaphal prefer sandy loam soil. This type of soil drains well, which is important for the roots of the trees. Good drainage helps prevent water from pooling around the roots, which can cause problems.

  • Planting

Farmers in udachiwadi mainly use seeds to grow custard apples. This method is cost-effective, making it suitable for small farms. Grafting is another method, but it is less common among local farmers.

  • Irrigation

While custards apple trees can survive with little waters, they need some irrigation when they are young. Farmers in Udachiwadi water the plants carefully during their early growth. once the trees are established, they need less water. Many farmers use drip irrigation systems to water the trees efficiently during important growth stages.

  • Challenges

Sitaphal can face problems from pests like fruit borers and diseases such as powdery mildew. Farmers manage these challenges using organic methods, like natural pestsides , to keep the crops healthy without relying on harmful chemicals.

Economic Impact

Demand and income custard apples are very popular because of their sweet taste and health benefits. In Udachiwadi , the production of Sitaphal helps increase local farmers income during the harvest season when they can sell their fruit for good prices.

Market Access

Farmers in Udachiwadi have improved their access to markets by connecting with near by towns .This allows them to sell their Sitaphal more easily and at better prices , which boosts their overall income and supports the local economy.
